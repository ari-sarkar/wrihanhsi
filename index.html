ABOUT US

     Our main purpose is to spread the light of education in every room of the society, at the base of the society we think that the people who are helpless and oppressed today turn to the light of the future, to turn away from the darkness. Change this society, this social system.

     We are helping 246 kids currently. We educate the poor children in the study as well as dance, draw, recite, etc. they are given educational materials as per their needs.

     In addition to this, a book bank has been created with the initiative of the Wrihanshi Foundation for the neglected children in society Where we collect old books or new books left in your home. Which we distribute among these children, We also collect color-pencils, rubber, etc. in our book bank.

     We also pass on some food collected through our Dry food bank (rice, pulses, root, pine, biscuits) every month to the poor abandoned elderly.
